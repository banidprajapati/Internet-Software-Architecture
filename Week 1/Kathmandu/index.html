About Kathmandu

Kathmandu is the capital and largest city of Nepal. It is located in the Kathmandu Valley and has a population of over 1 million people. The city is known for its rich history, culture, and stunning architecture, including ancient temples and palaces.

In addition to its cultural attractions, Kathmandu is also a hub for outdoor enthusiasts, with nearby mountains offering trekking, climbing, and other adventure sports. The city also has a lively food and drink scene, with traditional Nepali cuisine and local drinks that are not to be missed.

Whether you're a history buff, outdoor enthusiast, or just looking for a unique travel experience, Kathmandu has something to offer everyone. Start planning your trip today and discover all that this amazing city has to offer!
